## Core Principles (Expanded)

### 1. Vibecoding is Art
Just like modern art challenged what "art" could be, vibecoding challenges what "building" means. If you imagine something unique and bring it into existence with AI, you are an artist.

### 2. AI is Your Medium
A painter doesn't manufacture their paint. A sculptor doesn't mine their marble. You don't need to write every line of code. AI is your medium. How you direct it is your craft.

### 3. Patience is the Skill
The 90% that AI handles is not the skill. The 10% you push through — debugging, refining, iterating — that's where mastery lives. Patience isn't waiting. It's persisting.

### 4. Context is Everything
Most "loops" aren't AI limitations. They're prompt limitations. Learn to communicate clearly. Provide context. The better you describe, the better AI understands.

### 5. Ship > Perfect
A shipped project beats a perfect idea. Real users matter more than hypothetical features. Launch, learn, iterate.

### 6. Ignore the Gatekeepers
There will always be people who dismiss vibecoded work. They called photography "not art." They called digital art "not real." History proves them wrong every time.

### 7. Freedom > Safety
Traditional paths offer security. Vibecoding offers agency. Both are valid. Choose yours intentionally.

### 8. Proof Over Argument
Don't argue that vibecoding works. Show it. Every project is a statement. Every app is evidence. Let the work speak.

## The Division Zero Promise

We exist to:
- **Showcase** — Prove that vibecoded projects work
- **Inspire** — Show what's possible with AI + creativity
- **Educate** — Through vocabulary, tools, and community
- **Validate** — Every submission is recognition that you built something

We believe that the future belongs to those who can imagine and communicate clearly — not just those who can write syntax.
